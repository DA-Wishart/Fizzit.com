About Us

Fizzit.com sets its vision as the UK's easiest online trader of Books, CDs and DVDs. Decluttering some space and making some quick money has never been easier!

Getting started is as easy as entering a barcode or ISBN number, then check the price and once you have enough to qualify for our FREE delivery then trade. Sending us your stuff is as simple as putting it in any cardboard box and taking it to the local Post Office - no printing labels, no calling someone up for collection (hooray)!
